Fabric check — feel it first
✓ Good signs
  • Silk / satin — fluid, cool to touch
  • Fine cashmere / merino — soft, no scratch
  • Linen — structured, breathable
  • Quality cotton — substantial, not limp
  • Viscose / modal — fluid, drapes well
✕ Put it back
  • Polyester — shiny, static, feels cheap
  • Acrylic knitwear — scratchy, pills fast
  • Very thin cotton — see-through or limp
  • Stiff interfacing — won't drape
  • Strong chemical smell
